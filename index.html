About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate (Education, Concentration in Applied Linguistics), and I've made this website as a side project. Besides teaching EFL students, and I'm also having fun learning about data science and its applications in linguistics. This space will probably keep changing as I update my progress and add new stuff, so stay tuned!
